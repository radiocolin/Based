Please test this build with VoiceOver enabled.

Focus areas:

- Schedule screen
  Open several games and confirm each game card reads clearly with teams, score, status, and venue.
  Check the previous/next day controls and date picker announcement behavior.

- Game detail screen
  Move through the header, team picker, advisory banner, scorecard, timeline, and live at-bat panel.
  Confirm controls are announced clearly and activate as expected.

- Scorecard
  Swipe through batter rows and inning cells.
  Make sure VoiceOver explains which batter row you are on, which inning/plate appearance you are on, and whether a cell is the current live at-bat, an empty slot, or a totals/stat cell.
  Try both away and home scorecards.

- Timeline and live game state
  Check that each timeline item reads as one coherent summary instead of separate fragments.
  Confirm the current at-bat panel gives useful game context and opens details when activated.

- At-bat and player detail screens
  Open multiple at-bats and player cards.
  Verify the result, description, pitch sequence, and tappable player/pitcher names are announced correctly.

- Settings
  Test appearance, tint color, favorites management, and the attribution/footer area with VoiceOver.
  Confirm menu buttons, add/remove favorite actions, and row summaries are understandable.

Please report anything that is confusing, repetitive, missing context, or difficult to activate with VoiceOver.
