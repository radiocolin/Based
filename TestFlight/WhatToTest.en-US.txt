This is the final version of Based for the App Store.

Inning Transition Cleanup
- Watch a live game through a half-inning ending. The 3rd-out at-bat should appear in the scorecard within a few seconds of the "MID" or "END" break starting.
- Check the inning run totals in the scorecard footer row during breaks — they should show the correct run count immediately, even if the final at-bat cell hasn't filled in yet.
- If a run scores on the 3rd out (e.g. sac fly), the run should show in the inning total right away.

Network Resilience
- Put your phone in airplane mode for 30+ seconds during a live game, then re-enable. The scorecard should recover and catch up without needing to leave and re-enter the game.
- On a weak or spotty connection, the app should not freeze or hang. It should gracefully slow down and recover when signal returns.
- Lock your phone during a live game, wait 30+ seconds, then unlock. The scorecard should refresh immediately — you should not have to wait for the next update.
- Background the app for a minute, then return. Same thing — it should catch up right away.

Regression Checks
- At-bat presentation still works from scorecard, timeline, and live state.
- Team colors still readable in light and dark mode.
- Player detail still opens from batter and pitcher taps.
- Timeline footer still shows pitchers, umpires, game info.
- View mode persistence still remembers scorecard vs timeline.
