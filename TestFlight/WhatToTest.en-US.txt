New Teams tab

Browse all 30 MLB teams and their full season schedule. Favorites float to top.

- New "Teams" tab with pencil-styled baseball icon
- Team schedule: 162 games grouped by month, sticky stats header (record, W/L bar, home/away, streak, L10, run diff, 1-run games)
- Completed games show W/L + score; extras show inning (e.g. "W/10 5-4"). PPD games labeled. Next game highlighted.
- Series visually separated. Status bar tap scrolls to next game.
