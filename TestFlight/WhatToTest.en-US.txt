New Features: Auto-Entry & Manual Priority
- Added "Automatically enter live games" toggle for each favorite team.
- The app now automatically navigates to the highest-priority live game for a favorite team on launch or after returning from the background (5-minute runway).
- Favorite teams can now be manually reordered in Settings to prioritize them on the Schedule and for auto-entry selection.
- Updated Settings UI for favorites to a reorderable list with new per-team option sheets and plain ellipsis indicators.

Please focus this build on accessibility and Dynamic Type.

VoiceOver
- Turn VoiceOver on and move through the main tabs.
- Confirm the tab bar labels and icons are readable and understandable.
- On Schedule, swipe through game cards and confirm scheduled games do not announce fake scores.
- On Schedule, open the date picker and confirm focus moves into the calendar and back out correctly when dismissed.
- On Schedule, try the VoiceOver three-finger left and right swipe to move between days.
- Open a game and review the header, live state, timeline, footer, and scorecard.
- On the scorecard, confirm each cell gives enough context to understand the batter row, inning, and current location in the table.
- Open an at-bat detail view and confirm the sheet reads in a sensible order.
- In the at-bat pitch list, confirm pitch type, speed, and result are all spoken clearly.
- Open pitcher and batter detail sheets and confirm positions, handedness, and stat names are spoken naturally.
- In Settings, confirm rows, pickers, remove buttons, and the attribution footer all read cleanly.

Dynamic Type
- Test with larger text sizes, especially the largest Accessibility sizes in Settings > Accessibility > Display & Text Size > Larger Text.
- On Schedule, confirm cards grow enough to avoid team-name overlap or clipping at very large sizes.
- On Settings, confirm headers, descriptive copy, selector rows, and the Philadelphia LOVE attribution wrap correctly.
- Open the Appearance, Tint Color, and Add Favorite Team pickers and confirm text scales and remains readable.
- Open a player detail sheet and confirm both stat tables stay aligned without strange reflow or overflow.
- Open an at-bat detail sheet and confirm the sheet scrolls all the way to the pitch sequence at large sizes.
- In the at-bat pitch sequence, confirm large text switches to a readable wrapped layout with pitch type above speed.

General
- Report any place where text truncates, overlaps, becomes unreachable, or sounds unnatural in VoiceOver.
- Report any spot where focus lands in the wrong place, a control is announced as interactive but does nothing, or layout breaks at large text sizes.
